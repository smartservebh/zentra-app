Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident you'll love Zentra. That's why we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not completely satisfied with our service, you can request a full refund within 30 days of your initial purchase.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a refund:

  • You must request the refund within 30 days of your initial purchase
  • This applies only to your first paid subscription with Zentra
  • Your account must be in good standing (no violations of our Terms of Service)
  • Refunds are not available for renewal payments

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email our support team at support@zentrahub.pro
  2. Include your account email and order number
  3. Briefly explain why you're requesting a refund (optional but helpful)
  4. We'll process your request within 5-7 business days

Processing Time

Once your refund is approved:

  • We'll send you an email confirmation
  • The refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Credit card refunds typically appear within 5-10 business days
  • PayPal refunds are usually instant
  • Bank transfer refunds may take up to 14 business days

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Renewal payments after the initial subscription period
  • Partial month refunds for cancelled subscriptions
  • Add-on services or one-time purchases
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations

Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Log into your dashboard and go to Account Settings
  • Click on "Manage Subscription"
  • Select "Cancel Subscription"
  • Your access will continue until the end of your billing period

Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional situations may arise. If you have special circumstances that fall outside our standard refund policy, please contact us at support@zentra.pro. We'll review each case individually and do our best to find a fair solution.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact us:

  • Email: support@zentrahub.pro
  • Response time: Within 24 hours
