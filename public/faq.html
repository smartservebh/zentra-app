Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Zentra

General Questions

What is Zentra?

Zentra is an AI-powered platform that transforms your ideas into fully functional web applications using natural language descriptions in Arabic or English. No coding required!

How does Zentra work?

Simply describe your app idea in plain language (Arabic or English), and our AI will generate a complete, working web application with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. You can preview, edit, and publish your app instantly.

Do I need coding knowledge?

No! Zentra is designed for everyone. You don't need any programming experience. Just describe what you want, and we'll handle the technical details.

Pricing & Plans

Is there a free plan?

Yes! Our free plan includes 25 message credits and 500 integration credits per month, perfect for trying out Zentra and building small projects.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your plan at any time from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Technical Questions

What kind of apps can I build?

You can build any web application including portfolios, business websites, dashboards, tools, games, and more. Our AI understands complex requirements and creates responsive, modern applications.

Can I edit the generated code?

Yes! You have full access to the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code. You can edit it directly in our editor or download it for use in your preferred development environment.

Is hosting included?

Yes! All apps come with free hosting. You can publish your app instantly and share it with a public URL. Premium plans include custom domain support.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes! Starter plans and above support custom domains. Simply add your domain in the dashboard and follow our setup guide.

Support & Security

How can I get support?

Free users get community support through our forums. Paid plans include priority email support, and Pro plans get 24/7 dedicated support.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your apps and data are protected with enterprise-grade security measures.

Can I export my apps?

Yes! You can download your app's complete source code at any time. You own everything you create on Zentra.

What about privacy?

We take privacy seriously. We never share your personal information or app data with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
